Demand Level Guide

Low Demand Below 2,500 packs Within manageable limits. Standard allocation applies.
Moderate Demand 2,500 – 4,000 packs Monitor closely. May need minor allocation adjustment.
High Demand 4,000 – 6,000 packs Significant need. Pre-positioning recommended.
Critical Demand Above 6,000 packs Urgent action required. Immediate pre-positioning.
Demand levels are consistent with the Geospatial Map module color coding.
